Money Don't Grow on trees

Following Jesus Christ's instruction in Matthew:10:8, the United Church of God, an International Association , freely provides the gospel message to all who desire it. Donations to assist in proclaiming the gospel of the Kingdom of God to the world are gratefully accepted and are tax-deductible in the United States. Those who choose to voluntarily support this work are welcomed as coworkers in this effort to proclaim the true gospel to all nations. If you live outside the United States, please refer to our contact webpage for the office closest to you.
