About Serving

If you serve us, God will love you.

Trinity Church serves as a host congregation for the Interfaith Hospitality Network, operated by Family Promise, a nationwide organization that assists homeless and low-income families. The Parish provides housing for a week at a time on a rotating basis. Volunteers from the congregation and the local community prepare dinners for residents and stay at the Parish House overnight. For more about Family Promise, click here.

All who see Trinity as their spiritual home are asked to be regular in worship, to pray for the people and ministries of the Parish, to provide financial support, and to consider being involved in one or more Parish ministries. Members are encouraged to practice stewardship, or giving of time, talent and treasure to help the church fulfill its mission. This includes making an annual pledge, which helps the Vestry plan for revenue and spending. The Stewardship Committee holds a pledge drive each fall. Everyone is asked to consider giving a percentage of monthly income.
